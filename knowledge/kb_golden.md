## 1. Product Hardware & Design

**Q1: What are the key design differences between the G1 and G2 frames?**
**A:** The G2 features a significantly refined design compared to the G1, with temples that are **53% thinner** and a frame that is **20% narrower**, offering a sleeker and lighter profile.

**Q2: What materials are used for the G1 and G2 glasses?**
**A:** Both G1 and G2 frames are constructed from high-quality **magnesium alloy**, while the temples are made of **titanium alloy** combined with silicone components for durability and comfort.

**Q3: Are the G1 and G2 glasses heavy?**
**A:** Both models are designed to be lightweight for all-day wear, leveraging magnesium and titanium alloys to minimize weight without compromising strength.

**Q4: What color options are available for the G2 compared to G1?**
**A:** The G2 is available in three distinct colors: **Dark Grey**, **Olive Green**, and **Dark Brown**. (G1 color options would be specified here if different).

**Q5: How do I choose the right size for G1 or G2?**
**A:** Both models come in two sizes:
*   **Size A:** Features a wider bridge (~2mm wider), ideal for users with lower nose bridges.
*   **Size B:** Features a standard bridge but slightly wider lenses, suitable for users with wider face shapes.

**Q6: Do G1 and G2 use the same hinge mechanism?**
**A:** Yes, both G1 and G2 utilize a **patented screwless hinge** design, ensuring durability and a clean aesthetic.

**Q7: Why is there a green glow on the lenses of my G1/G2?**
**A:** This is a normal optical phenomenon common to both G1 and G2, caused by the diffractive grating technology used in the waveguide display. It is inherent to the technology, not a defect.

**Q8: My G1/G2 makes a hissing sound when I clean the lenses. Is this normal?**
**A:** Yes, for both models. The "hissing" or "squeaking" sound results from the friction between the cleaning cloth and the unique **micro-nano texture** of the lenses. It is normal and decreases with use.

**Q9: Can I adjust the nose pads on the G1 and G2?**
**A:** Yes, both the G1 and G2 feature **adjustable nose pads** to help you achieve a comfortable and secure fit.

**Q10: Why does the display on my G1/G2 look yellow in snowy conditions?**
**A:** This effect occurs on both models due to **blue light diffraction** (430-470nm) by the waveguides. In bright, white environments like snow, the remaining light spectrum shifts towards yellow.

## 2. Display & Optics

**Q11: What display technology is used in the G1 versus the G2?**
**A:** Both G1 and G2 use **binocular Micro LED** displays with optical waveguides for high-efficiency, transparent visuals.

**Q12: Is the display on G1/G2 color or monochrome?**
**A:** Both models feature a **monochromatic green** display, chosen for its superior visibility and energy efficiency.

**Q13: How bright is the display on the G1 and G2?**
**A:** Both G1 and G2 displays peak at **1200 nits**, ensuring the green Micro LED visuals are clearly visible even in strong outdoor sunlight.

**Q14: What is the field of view (FoV) for the G1 and G2?**
**A:** Both models offer a field of view of **27.5 degrees**.

**Q15: What is the display resolution for G1 and G2?**
**A:** Both G1 and G2 have a resolution of **640 x 350 pixels**.

**Q16: Do G1 and G2 have the same refresh rate?**
**A:** Yes, both displays operate at a **60Hz refresh rate**.

**Q17: What is the light transmittance of the G1/G2 lenses?**
**A:** Both models have **98% light transmittance**, ensuring they look and function like regular high-quality glasses.

**Q18: Do G1 and G2 lenses block UV rays?**
**A:** Yes, lenses on both the G1 and G2 provide **UV400 protection**.

**Q19: Can I adjust the display brightness on G1 and G2?**
**A:** Both models feature **auto-brightness control**. You can also manually adjust brightness via voice commands using **Even AI**.

**Q20: Why does text look cut off at the corners on my G1/G2?**
**A:** This issue can occur on both models due to frame fitting. **Frame A**, with its wider bridge, might sit differently on your face. Try adjusting the nose pads or the temple angle to align the display.

## 3. Battery & Charging

**Q21: How does the battery life compare between G1 and G2?**
**A:**
*   **G1:** Has a 160mAh battery.
*   **G2:** Has a larger **192mAh** battery.
Both typically last up to **2 days** with regular use, but G2 offers slightly more capacity.

**Q22: How long does it take to charge the G1 versus the G2?**
**A:** Both models take approximately **2.5 hours** to charge from 0% to 100%.

**Q23: Can I use my G1 charging case for my G2 glasses?**
**A:** **No.** They are strictly incompatible.
*   **G1:** Uses **wireless charging** inside the case.
*   **G2:** Uses **metal contact charging**.
You must use the specific case designed for your model.

**Q24: How many times can the case recharge the G1/G2?**
**A:** Both the G1 and G2 charging cases (2000mAh) can recharge the glasses approximately **2.5 times** when fully charged.

**Q25: Are the charging cases for G1/G2 waterproof?**
**A:** No, neither case is waterproof. For the G2 specifically, ensure the **metal contacts** are kept dry to prevent circuit damage.

**Q26: What adapter should I use for G1/G2 cases?**
**A:** For both models, we recommend using a **5V 1.5A** or higher USB-C adapter.

**Q27: How do I check the battery level on G1 and G2?**
**A:** For both models, you can check the battery level on the **"Dashboard"** inside the glasses or via the **Even App**.

**Q28: Does the G1 have metal contacts for charging?**
**A:** No, the **G1 relies on wireless charging**. Only the **G2** features metal contacts on the frame for charging.

**Q29: How long does the R1 Ring battery last?**
**A:** The Even R1 Ring (compatible with G2 only) lasts between **4 to 7 days** under regular usage.

**Q30: How is the R1 Ring charged?**
**A:** The R1 uses **NFC wireless charging**. Place it on its base with the "Even logo" facing the charging cable.

**Q33: Does the G1 support "Teleprompt"?**
**A:** Yes, both G1 and G2 support the **Teleprompt** feature for displaying scripts.

**Q34: Does "Translate" work differently on G1 and G2?**
**A:** The core function is similar, but **G2 supports more displayable languages** (e.g., Cantonese) than G1. (See Language Support section).

*   **G2 Only:** G2 **can display** Cantonese and Greek text.

**Q36: Does the G1 support Cantonese text display?**
**A:** **No.** On G1, Cantonese is a "Restricted Speech Input" language (App display only). On **G2**, Cantonese text **can** be displayed on the glasses.

**Q37: What is "Even AI" and is it on both models?**
**A:** Yes, **Even AI** is available on both G1 and G2. It can answer questions and control device settings via voice commands.

**Q38: Do both G1 and G2 support navigation?**
**A:** Yes, both models support the **Navigate** feature, powered by Google Maps data from your paired phone.

**Q39: Can I filter notifications on both G1 and G2?**
**A:** Yes, for both models, you can customize which apps (e.g., WhatsApp, Slack) send notifications to your glasses via the Even App.

**Q40: How do I scroll on the G1 vs G2?**
**A:**
*   **G1:** Swipe forward/backward on the **glasses temple**.
*   **G2:** You can use the **glasses temple** OR the **R1 Ring** (swipe up/down) to scroll.

## 6. Prescription Lenses

**Q46: Can I order progressive lenses online for G1 or G2?**
**A:** **No.** For both models, online orders support **Single Vision (Distance)** only. Progressive lenses require professional fitting at an **Even Retail Store**.

**Q47: Is Prism correction available online for G1/G2?**
**A:** **No.** Prism lenses for both G1 and G2 are available **only** at authorized **Even Retail Stores**.

**Q48: What is the supported power range for online orders (G1 & G2)?**
**A:** **Sphere (SPH):** +8.00 to -12.00 D.
**Cylinder (CYL):** +4.00 to -4.00 D.
This applies to both models.

**Q49: Can I order reading glasses (ADD power) online?**
**A:** **No.** For both G1 and G2, online orders **ignore ADD values**. The optical display is optimized for distance/intermediate vision, not primary reading.

**Q50: Which lens index should I choose for high myopia (-7.00 D)?**
**A:** For both G1 and G2, we recommend **Index 1.74** for prescriptions with an SE < -6.00 D (High Myopia) or > +4.00 D (High Hyperopia).

**Q51: Can I replace the lenses myself on G1 or G2?**
**A:** **Absolutely not.** For both G1 and G2, the lenses are integrated with the optical display module. Unauthorized replacement will **damage the device and void the warranty**.

**Q52: How do I update my prescription for G1 or G2?**
**A:** Contact Support. For both models, this is a **paid custom service** requiring the glasses to be returned to the factory for professional installation.

**Q53: Can I return prescription G1/G2 glasses?**
**A:** **No.** Prescription glasses for both models are custom-made and **non-returnable** once production starts (24 hours after order).

**Q54: What if my G1/G2 prescription lenses are defective?**
**A:** For both models, we offer a free replacement or repair within **30 days** of delivery specifically for manufacturing defects or installation errors.
