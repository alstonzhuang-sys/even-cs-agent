# Even Realities Official Policies (Warranty, Return, Shipping)

This document contains the authoritative policy text for Warranty, Returns, Shipping, and Privacy.

## 1. Warranty Policy

### Scope of Application
* **Coverage:** Products purchased directly from Even Realities official channels or authorized retailers.
* **Period:** One (1) year Limited Warranty (Two years for EU/EFTA). Commences on delivery date.
* **Proof:** Valid purchase proof (order number) required.
* **Coverage:** Main unit and charging case accessories only. **Excludes:** accessories, consumables (protective films, cases), software, third-party services.
* **Data:** User responsibility to remove data before return.

### What Is Covered
* Defects/malfunctions not caused by human factors.
* Repair, replacement (new/refurbished), or software update.
* **Lens Defects:** Free replacement/repair within 30 days of delivery for manufacturing defects or improper installation.
* **Logistics:** Even Realities covers costs for quality issues; User covers costs for user-caused issues.

### What Is NOT Covered
* Scratches resulting from use.
* Unauthorized lens installation/replacement.
* Liquid damage to charging contacts.
* Consumable components (batteries) unless defect-caused.
* Misuse, abuse, accidents, unauthorized servicing.
* Removed/tampered labels.

### Procedures
* **Service Extension:** Warranty period extended by retention duration.
* **Refund:** Partial refund if repair/replacement is unreasonable.
* **Void Claims:** User pays return shipping or product disposed of after 30 days.

## 2. Return Policy

### Eligible Return Situations
* **Non-Customized Products:** Return for refund within **14 days** of receiving order. Deduct shipping/handling if not quality-related.
* **Consumables:** Nose pads/Temple sleeves non-returnable once unsealed.
* **Prescription Glasses:** Custom-made. **NOT eligible for return or cancellation once 24 hours have passed** after order placement.
* **Ring Products (R1):** Free one-time model replacement within **14 days** for size issues.
* **Condition:** Must be "like new", no visible signs of use. Original packaging intact (can be opened but undamaged).
* **Bundles:** Refund value deducted by bundle discount if partial return.

### Process
1. Contact support@evenrealities.com.
2. Receive return instructions/label.
3. Ship in original packaging.
4. Inspection -> Refund to original payment method.
5. **Non-Refundable:** Shipping fees, handling charges, VAT/customs fees.
