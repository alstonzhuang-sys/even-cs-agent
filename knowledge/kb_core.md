# Even Realities Product Core Facts (Specs & Price)

This document contains the authoritative hardware specifications, pricing, and core parameters for Even Realities products (G1, G2, R1).

## Even G1 Specs & Price

### Materials & Hardware
* **Frame Materials:** Magnesium alloy, Titanium alloy, and Silicone.
* **Nose Pad Materials:** Titanium support with adjustable nose pads.
* **Display Lifespan:** Cumulative light-on time of 20,000 hours.

### Protection & Standards
* **Dust & Water Resistance:** IP55 Certified (avoid direct water exposure).
* **UV Index (Clip & Lenses):** UV400.
* **Lens Features:** Edge polishing, High refractive index lens, Scratch-resistant coating.
* **Abrasion Resistance Standards:** ISO 4618 (International); GB/T 23989, GB/T 6739 (China).
* **Radiation Safety (EMF):** Complies with CE/FCC certification (radiation levels significantly lower than TWS Bluetooth earbuds).
* **Privacy Compliance:** Complies with EU GDPR standards.
* **Legal Compliance:** Meets US FDA and EU MDR filing requirements; registered with UK MHRA.

### Power & Charging
* **Battery Capacity (Glasses):** 160 mAh / 0.616 Wh.
* **Charging Cable Type:** USB-C to USB-C.
* **Adapter Recommendation:** 5V 1.5A or higher.
* **Charging Duration (Glasses):** ~2.5 hours for 0% to 100%.
* **Charging Case:**
 * Battery: 2000 mAh / 7.4 Wh / 3.7V.
 * Recharging Capability: Fully charged case charges glasses 2.5 times.
 * Cycles: Capacity ≥ 80% after 300 cycles.
* **Battery Health:** Glasses capacity degrades to ~80% after 500 full cycles.

### Connectivity
* **Bluetooth:** BLE 5.2.
* **Data Transfer Rate:** Up to 2Mbps.
* **Connection Distance:** ~80m (Samsung S24), 30-35m (iPhone 15).

### Audio & Microphone
* **Effective Pickup Distance:** 2 meters (45-60dB), 3 meters (60-70dB).
* **Ambient Noise Limit:** >85dB severely interferes with pickup.
* **Pickup Switching:** Switch between glasses/phone mic in App.
* **Third-party Support:** No external microphone support.

### Hardware Knowledge (FAQ)
* **Microphone Pickup:** 2m (Standard), 3m (Quiet). Filters noise <85dB.
* **Lens Cleaning:** "Hissing/squeaking" sound is normal (micro-nano texture).
* **Visuals:** Green glow/Yellow tint (in snow) is normal optical diffraction.

### Language Support
* **System UI:** English, Deutsch, Français, Español, Italiano, Japan, Chinese.
* **Core Functions (Translate, Transcribe, QuickNote, Teleprompter):** Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, German, French, Italian, Spanish, Dutch, Danish, Finnish, Norwegian, Swedish, Polish, Portuguese, Turkish, Russian, Ukrainian, Hungarian, Czech.
* **Restricted Speech Input (Translate only):**
 * **Languages:** Arabic, Bengali, Cantonese, Hindi, Greek.
 * **Limitation:** Supports voice input as source language only; **cannot be displayed as text on glasses** (can only be translated into other displayable languages).

## Even G2 Specs & Price

### Dimensions
* **Sizes:** Size A (wider bridge), Size B (wider lenses).

### Frame & Design
* **Colors:** Dark grey, Olive green, Dark brown.
* **Materials:** Magnesium alloy (Frame), Titanium alloy (Temple).
* **Hinge:** Patented screwless hinge.
* **Bridge:** Universal fit.
* **Nose Pads:** Adjustable.

### Lens & Display Technology
* **Lens Color:** Clear.
* **UV Index:** UV400.
* **Lens Features:** Edge polishing, High refractive index, Scratch-resistant, Anti-reflective, Anti-fingerprint.
* **Prescription Material:** MR™ Series (Mitsui Chemicals).
* **Optical Technology:** Waveguides.
* **Display Mode:** Binocular.
* **Light Transmittance:** 98%.
* **Resolution:** 640 * 350.
* **FoV:** 27.5°.
* **Refresh Rate:** 60Hz.
* **Peak Brightness:** 1200 nits.
* **Display Type:** Micro LED (Green).

### Capabilities
* **Microphones:** Four Microphones.
* **Brightness Control:** Auto.
* **Bluetooth:** BLE 5.4.
* **Core Functions:** Conversate, Teleprompt, Translate, Navigate, Notification, Even AI, Silent Mode.
* **Language Support:**
 * **System UI & Core Functions:** Same as G1.
 * **Restricted Speech Input (Translate only):**
 * **Languages:** Arabic, Bengali, Hindi.
 * **Note:** Cantonese and Greek are **fully supported** (displayable) on G2.
 * **Limitation:** Restricted languages support voice input only; **no text display on glasses**.

### Battery & Power
* **Glasses Battery:** 192mAh / 0.744Wh.
* **Charging Case Battery:** 2000mAh / 7.4Wh.
* **Total Battery Life:** Up to 2 days regular use.
* **Charging Method:** Metal contacts (NOT compatible with G1).

### Compliance
* **Certifications:** FCC, IC, RF standards.
* **Water Resistance:** IP65 (Rain/splashes ok, no immersion/swimming).

### Internal Notes [INTERNAL ONLY]
* **Sizing:** Model A (wider bridge, lower nose bridges); Model B (wider lenses, wider faces).
* **Visibility:** Green display visible in strong light due to 1200 nits.
* **G1/G2 Incompatibility:** G1 (wireless) and G2 (metal contacts) cases are NOT compatible.
* **Visual Differences:** G2 temples 53% thinner, frame 20% narrower than G1.
* **Ring Compatibility:** R1 currently ONLY compatible with G2.

## Even R1 Specs & Price

### Materials & Design
* **Outer:** Grey ceramic (matte metallic finish).
* **Inner:** Stainless steel (PVD coating, hypoallergenic).
* **Sensor Packaging:** Epoxy resin.
* **Sizes:** 6 - 15.
* **Wearing Detection:** Supported.

### Sensors & Health
* **SpO2:** Red light sensor.
* **Heart Rate / Sleep / HRV:** Green light + Infrared PPG.
* **Activity:** IMU sensor.
* **Temperature:** NTC sensor (Relative trends ONLY, not absolute values).

### Power & Charging
* **Battery Life:** 4 - 7 days.
* **Charging:** NFC wireless (magnetic alignment).
* **Duration:** 20 - 90 minutes.
* **Input:** 5V DC, <15W.

### Connectivity & Protection
* **Bluetooth:** Stable connection to phone or G2.
* **Waterproof:** IP68 (Swimming/showering OK).

### Internal Notes [INTERNAL ONLY]
* **Temperature:** Emphasize "trends" over clinical thermometer use. **Not a medical device.**
* **Waterproof:** Explicitly IP68 (higher than glasses).
