# Prescription Lens Knowledge Module

## What is Even Prescription Lenses?
Digitally Surfaced Lenses customized based on individual eye exams. Corrects Myopia, Hyperopia, and Astigmatism.

## Prescription Parameters
*   **SPH (Sphere):** +/- Power. (Myopia -, Hyperopia +). Range: **+12.00 to -12.00 D**.
*   **CYL (Cylinder):** Astigmatism power. Range: **+4.00 to -4.00 D**.
*   **AXIS:** Orientation (0-180°). **Required** if CYL exists.
*   **ADD (Addition):** Near vision power. **Not supported for online orders.** Contact an Even Retail Store.
*   **PD (Pupillary Distance):** Distance between pupil centers (typ. 60-66mm).

## Lens Index Selection Guide
Calculate **Spherical Equivalent (SE) = SPH + ½ CYL**.

| Index | Recommended SE Range | Characteristics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1.60** | SE > -3.00 D | Thin, economic. Suitable for medium power. |
| **1.67** | -3.00 D to -6.00 D | Thinner, lighter. Suitable for high prescriptions. |
| **1.74** | **SE < -6.00 D** OR **SE > +4.00 D** | Ultra-thin, premium. Best for very high Myopia or Hyperopia. |

## Supported Prescription Types
*   **Single Vision (Distance):** Supported **Online** & **In-Store**.
*   **Progressive Lenses:** **In-Store Only**. (Requires professional fitting for transition zones).
*   **Prism Lenses:** **In-Store Only**. (Requires professional measurement for alignment).
*   **Reading Glasses:** **In-Store Only**. (Note: Display optics are optimized for intermediate-far distances, not primary reading).
