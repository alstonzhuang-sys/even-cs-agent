# Even Realities Product Manual & Troubleshooting Guide

## 1. Even G2 Features

### Menu & Navigation
*   **Launch:** Long-press TouchPad (1s).
*   **Navigate:** Swipe forward/backward (glasses) or up/down (ring).
*   **Select:** Tap TouchPad.
*   **Functions:** Dashboard, Conversate, Teleprompt, Translate, Navigate, Notification, Even AI, Silent Mode.

### Dashboard
*   **Status:** Time, Date, Weather, Battery, Unread Msgs.
*   **Widgets:** News, Stocks, Calendar, QuickList, Health (R1 only).
*   **Manage:** Customize via App.
*   **Expand:** Click TouchPad.

### Conversate (Real-time Assistance)
*   **Requires:** Internet, Mic Permission.
*   **Interface:**
    *   **AI Summary (Top):** Core topic.
    *   **AI Cues (Left):** Concepts, Bios, Answers, Suggestions.
    *   **Transcripts (Bottom):** Real-time text.
*   **Control:** Double-tap to end. Action Items saved to QuickList.

### Translate (Bilingual)
*   **Modes:** Full-Screen (Original + Translated) / Half-Screen (Translated Only). Toggle via Tap.
*   **Supported Languages:** 30+ (Chinese, English, Spanish, etc.).
*   **Restricted:** Arabic, Bengali, Hindi (App display only, **no glasses display**).

### Teleprompt
*   **Modes:**
    *   **AI Mode:** Voice-Follow scrolling.
    *   **Auto Mode:** Steady speed.
    *   **Manual Mode:** Tap-to-page (Touch strip/Volume keys).
*   **Formats:** TXT, DOCX, PDF (Simple layout). Max 255KB.
*   **Controller:** R1 Ring or Bluetooth Controller (Volume keys).

### Navigate
*   **Source:** Google Maps.
*   **Launch:** App (Search) or Menu (Favorites only).
*   **Modes:**
    *   **Mini Map:** Heads-Up (Rotates with you).
    *   **Full Map:** North-Up (Fixed).
*   **Note:** App must be foreground/unlocked for full updates. Cached area works offline.

### Notification
*   **Settings:** Auto Display (Pop-up), Manual (Menu).
*   **Action:** Tap to Read/Close. Double-tap to Dismiss.
*   **Apps:** Toggle per app in Even App settings.

### Even AI
*   **Trigger:** "Hey Even" or Menu.
*   **Capabilities:** Full-Duplex (Interruptible), Multi-turn context.
*   **Skills:** Control Brightness, Teleprompt, Navigate, etc.

## 2. Even R1 Health Features (Health Center)
*   **Metrics:** Steps, Calories, Sleep (Stages), Heart Rate, HRV, SpO2, Temperature (Relative Trends).
*   **Productivity Score:** 0-100 scale.
*   **Temperature Note:** Measures *variance* from baseline, not absolute fever temp. **Not to be used as a clinical medical thermometer.**

## 3. Setup & Pairing
*   **App:** Even Realities App (iOS 14+, Android 11+).
*   **Pairing:** "Add a device" -> Search -> Connect.
*   **Multi-Device:** One phone = One G2 + One R1 active.
*   **Reset:** Factory Reset in App to erase settings. "Remove from Account" to unbind.

## 4. Hardware FAQ & Troubleshooting

### Audio
*   **Pickup Distance:** 2m (Standard), 3m (Quiet).
*   **Noise:** Filters background noise <85dB.

### Visuals
*   **Green Glow:** Normal optical phenomenon (diffractive grating). Minimized by design.
*   **Yellow Tint:** Normal in bright light/snow. Blue light diffraction (430-470nm) shifts remaining light warmer.
*   **"Cut-Corner" Effect (G2):**
    *   **Frame A:** Wider bridge (~2mm), lower out-coupling. Better for high nose bridges.
    *   **Frame B:** Standard.
    *   **Fix:** Adjust wearing position or switch frames.

### Maintenance
*   **Cleaning Sound:** Hissing/squeaking when wiping lens is normal (micro-nano texture). Decreases with use.

### Connectivity
*   **Outlook Sync:** Sync Outlook to *System Calendar* first (iOS/Android settings).
*   **Logs:** Settings -> Feedback -> "Authorize Log Upload" -> Submit.

## 5. Compatibility
*   **G1 vs G2 App:** Separate apps. Can exist on same phone.
*   **R1 with G1:** **Not Compatible.** R1 is G2-exclusive.
*   **Source Code:** Not open source. No GitHub fork.
*   **G1 Conversate:** Not available (Hardware limitation).
